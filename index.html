Standard Service


We are located in chicago, IL and we ship package to Mexico, once a month, and date will be declared before the time. We only consider the dimensions of shipping boxes, it won't matter how much does it weigh. We basically offer three types of shipping services with different prices. Standard service is most preferable and best price, you can come to our facility and drop your box and get the tracking information.
